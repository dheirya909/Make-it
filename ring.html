What is the Nexus Ring?

The Nexus Ring is a smart wearable that pairs with every Metro Nexus pod. Tap to board, tap to exit — the pod senses exactly where you get on and where you step off, then charges you only for the distance you actually travelled. No scanners, no gates, no fumbling for a phone. Just walk in and walk out.

01 — Boarding

Tap-free boarding

The pod detects your ring the moment you step inside and links the ride to your account automatically.

02 — Sensing

Exit & location sensing

On-board sensors read where you leave the pod, so you're billed only for the segment you actually rode.

03 — Billing

Charged on exit, not on book

Booking a ride holds nothing. Your wallet is debited only once the pod confirms you've disembarked.

04 — Identity

One identity across the line

Your ring is your wallet, your pass and your live pod tracker — all without taking out your phone.
